Phase 1: YouTube Channel Setup

  • Create YouTube Brand Account High
    Sign in to YouTube with the Norris Utilities Google account. Create a Brand Account (not a personal channel) so multiple team members can manage it. Set the channel name to "Norris Utilities" with the handle @NorrisUtilities.
  • Upload Channel Art & Profile Photo High
    Profile photo: Norris Utilities Phoenix Icon® logo on dark navy background (800x800px). Banner: Blue gradient header with "NORRIS UTILITIES" and tagline "A Legacy of Commitment®" (2560x1440px). Coordinate with MK Smith for final design assets.
  • Write Channel Description & About Section Medium
    Include: Norris Utilities®, LLC — Birmingham, AL. Third-generation utility equipment dealer/distributor. Authorized distributor of FlexPro Armor bucket covers, Samson Rope, Skylift bucket trucks, and Bay Shore Systems drill rigs. Serving 20+ states. Contact: 205-500-1343 | acnorris@norrisutilities.com | www.NorrisUtilities.com
  • Configure Channel Settings Medium
    Add website link (www.NorrisUtilities.com), business email, and location (Birmingham, AL). Set default upload settings: category "Science & Technology," tags for utility equipment, bucket trucks, lineman tools, FlexPro Armor. Enable channel customization and featured sections.
  • Create Channel Sections & Playlists Low
    Set up initial playlists: "FlexPro Armor Bucket Covers," "Equipment Demos," "Highway & Field Videos," and "Customer Testimonials." Organize the channel homepage with featured video, recent uploads, and playlist sections.

Phase 2: Highway Video Production

  • Plan Highway Video Content High
    Outline the highway video: capture linemen and bucket trucks working along highways, demonstrating real-world utility equipment in action. Show FlexPro Armor bucket covers installed on trucks, Samson Rope in use, and crew safety in the field. Target length: 60–90 seconds for social media, 3–5 minutes for full YouTube version.
  • Schedule & Coordinate Shoot High
    Identify a customer jobsite or utility crew willing to be filmed (get written permission). Coordinate timing with MK Smith for any professional videography needs. Ensure proper safety distance and PPE compliance during filming. Bring FlexPro Armor product samples for close-up shots.
  • Film Highway Video High
    Capture footage: wide highway shots of bucket trucks in action, close-ups of FlexPro Armor covers on buckets, crews working safely, and Norris Utilities branding on equipment. Record in landscape (16:9) at 1080p minimum, 4K preferred. Get b-roll of equipment, signage, and product details.
  • Edit & Brand the Video Medium
    Add Norris Utilities® intro/outro with Phoenix Icon® branding. Include lower-thirds with product names and contact info. Background music: professional, industrial feel. End card: "Norris Utilities® — A Legacy of Commitment®" with phone number 205-500-1343 and website. Coordinate with MK Smith on final edit.
  • Upload & Optimize on YouTube Medium
    Upload the final video to the Norris Utilities channel. Write an SEO-optimized title and description with keywords: utility equipment, bucket truck covers, FlexPro Armor, lineman safety, Norris Utilities. Add a custom thumbnail with the Norris Utilities brand header style. Set as featured video on the channel homepage. Share to LinkedIn and send link to key customers.
