Action Required

Follow Up on Remaining Inventory from Last Purchase Order

Captured April 12, 2026 — Norris Utilities® Action Tracker
Priority: High Inventory & Fulfillment

Objective

Contact the supplier to confirm the status of all outstanding items on the most recent purchase order. Determine expected delivery dates for any inventory that has not yet arrived and verify that the full order will be fulfilled as originally placed.

Follow-Up Checklist

  • Pull the most recent PO number and review line items for any outstanding quantities
  • Contact the supplier directly — phone call preferred, email follow-up for documentation
  • Confirm which items have shipped and request tracking numbers if available
  • Get estimated delivery dates for any items still on backorder or pending production
  • Ask whether any items have been discontinued or substituted since the PO was placed
  • Confirm pricing matches original PO — flag any cost changes
  • Document all responses and update inventory tracking records

Key Information to Gather

PO Status
Partial / Complete / Backordered
Expected Arrival
Get specific dates from supplier
Pricing Confirmation
Match against original PO amounts
Tracking Numbers
Request for any shipped items

Notes

Keeping inventory levels consistent is critical to fulfilling customer orders on time. If any items are delayed beyond the original lead time, evaluate whether to source from an alternate supplier or adjust customer commitments accordingly.

After follow-up is complete, update the internal inventory tracker and notify Caroline Butler if any customer orders are affected by delays.
