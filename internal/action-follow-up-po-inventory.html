Action Item

Follow Up on Last PO Order for Inventory

Created: April 13, 2026  |  Owner: Aaron C. Norris

Objective

Contact suppliers to confirm the status of the most recent purchase order(s) placed for inventory replenishment. Verify expected delivery dates, confirm quantities shipped, and flag any backorders or delays that could impact fulfillment.

Follow-Up Checklist

  • Pull the most recent PO number(s) from order records
  • Contact the supplier to confirm shipment status and tracking
  • Verify quantities shipped match the PO quantities ordered
  • Confirm expected delivery date to Norris Utilities® warehouse
  • Ask about any backorders, partial shipments, or substitutions
  • Request updated lead times for any items not yet shipped
  • Update inventory tracking with confirmed delivery estimates
  • Flag any discrepancies or issues for immediate resolution
