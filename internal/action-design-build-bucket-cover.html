Build Process

1

Measure & Template

Confirm bucket dimensions against SKU specifications. Verify customer’s truck make/model if custom sizing is needed. Create or pull existing cutting template for the appropriate SKU.

2

Material Selection & Cut

Select heavy-duty UV-resistant vinyl from approved stock. Cut panels according to template dimensions, adding seam allowance for double-stitched construction.

3

Assembly & Stitching

Sew panels with double-reinforced seams. Install elastic drawstring channel along bottom edge. For combo covers: attach integrated pocket panels before final closure.

4

Hardware & Finishing

Install cord lock on drawstring. Apply brand label. Inspect all seams under tension. Trim excess material and heat-seal raw edges to prevent fraying.

5

Quality Check & Ship

Final dimensional verification against SKU spec sheet. Fit-test on mock bucket form. Package, label with correct SKU, and prepare for shipment.

Quality Checklist

  • Dimensions match SKU specification within ½" tolerance
  • All seams double-stitched with no skipped stitches
  • Drawstring channel runs continuous with no gaps
  • Cord lock engages and holds under load
  • No raw/unsealed edges visible
  • Cover fits snugly on test form without bunching or excess slack
  • Brand label centered and securely attached
  • Packaged with correct SKU label and shipping documentation

Pricing Formula
