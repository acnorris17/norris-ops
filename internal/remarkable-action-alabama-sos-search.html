Search Alabama Secretary of State for LLCs Containing "Golan" or "Peled"

Search the Alabama Secretary of State business entity database for any LLCs or other registered entities that contain the name "Golan" or "Peled" in their filings. Document all results including entity name, formation date, status, registered agent, and principal address.
Search Terms
"Golan"
"Peled"
Where to Search

Alabama Secretary of State — Business Entity Search

Use the Alabama SOS online business entity search portal to look up all registered LLCs, corporations, and other entities containing the keywords "Golan" and "Peled" in the entity name or registered agent fields.

Website: sos.alabama.gov — Business Entity Records

Steps to Complete
  • Go to the Alabama Secretary of State business entity search at sos.alabama.gov
  • Search for all entities containing "Golan" — record every result (name, status, formation date, registered agent, address)
  • Search for all entities containing "Peled" — record every result (name, status, formation date, registered agent, address)
  • Note any entities where Golan and Peled appear together or share the same registered agent or address
  • Check entity status: Active, Inactive, or Dissolved
  • Cross-reference any shared principals, officers, or organizers between the entities found
  • Document all findings and report back to Aaron
Record Findings

What to Capture for Each Entity

For every LLC or entity found, record the following:

• Entity Name — full legal name as registered

• Entity ID Number — the SOS filing number

• Formation Date — when it was filed

• Status — Active, Inactive, or Dissolved

• Registered Agent — name and address

• Principal Address — listed business address

• Organizers / Officers — any names listed in the filing
