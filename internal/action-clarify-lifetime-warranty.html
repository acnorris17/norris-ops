FlexPro Armor Lifetime Warranty

Defining "Lifetime" — Internal Decision Reference

THE CORE QUESTION

When Norris Utilities® says FlexPro Armor bucket covers come with a "lifetime warranty," does "lifetime" mean the life of the bucket/truck (the structure the cover is installed on) or the life of the original purchaser (the customer who bought it)?

Two Definitions Compared

LOWER RISK, STRONGER BRAND

Option A: Life of the Original Purchaser

The warranty covers FlexPro Armor bucket covers for as long as the original purchaser owns and uses the product. Warranty does not transfer if the cover is resold or given to another party.

  • Clear end point — warranty ends when original buyer sells or stops using the cover
  • Industry standard — most manufacturers (tools, equipment, outdoor gear) define lifetime this way
  • Non-transferable keeps exposure predictable and rewards the customer who trusted you directly
  • Eliminates disputes from secondhand buyers claiming warranty on used covers
  • Easier to administer — original invoice = proof of warranty eligibility
HIGHER RISK, BOLD PROMISE

Option B: Life of the Structure

The warranty covers FlexPro Armor bucket covers for as long as the bucket truck or aerial device they were originally installed on remains in active service.

  • Bucket trucks can stay in service 15–25+ years — long warranty tail
  • Trucks change owners multiple times, creating warranty transfer disputes
  • Hard to verify — how do you confirm the original truck is still in service?
  • Opens the door to claims from buyers you never sold to
  • Could be used as a selling point, but the liability may outweigh the marketing value

Industry Benchmarks

Company / Product Warranty Definition "Lifetime" Means
YETI Coolers Limited Lifetime Life of original purchaser, non-transferable

Recommended Warranty Language

Draft Warranty Statement for FlexPro Armor Bucket Covers

FlexPro Armor Limited Lifetime Warranty


Norris Utilities®, LLC warrants all FlexPro Armor bucket covers against defects in materials and workmanship for the lifetime of the original purchaser. This warranty is non-transferable and applies only to covers purchased directly from Norris Utilities® or an authorized Norris Utilities® dealer.


What is covered: Manufacturing defects, stitching failure, material breakdown under normal commercial use, and hardware/fastener defects.


What is not covered: Damage from misuse, modification, chemical exposure, improper installation, normal wear and tear (abrasion, UV fading over extended use), or use on equipment the cover was not designed for.


How to make a claim: Contact Norris Utilities® at 205-500-1343 or acnorris@norrisutilities.com with your original invoice and photos of the issue. If the cover is defective, Norris Utilities® will repair or replace it at no charge.


"Lifetime" defined: The useful life of the product for the original purchaser. This warranty does not guarantee the product will last forever — it guarantees that if the product fails due to our manufacturing, we will make it right.
