Steps to Complete
  1. Access Jefferson County Probate Court records (online portal or in-person at the Jefferson County Courthouse, Birmingham, AL)
  2. Search deed records for 2018 transfers from Compass Bank (now BBVA / PNC) to McKnight
  3. Locate the specific instrument number and recording date for the deed
  4. Request a certified copy of the deed from the Probate Office
  5. Save digital copy and file the certified original with property records
Notes

Compass Bank was acquired by BBVA USA in 2019, which was subsequently acquired by PNC Financial Services in 2021. Records from 2018 should still be filed under "Compass Bank" in the probate index. If searching online, also check under the grantor name "BBVA Compass" as some late-2018 recordings may have used the transitional name. The Jefferson County Probate Court online search is available during business hours.
