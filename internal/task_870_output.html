Get Patent Drawings

Captured April 13, 2026 — from reMarkable notes
Pending Action

What Needs to Happen

Obtain the patent drawings required for the intellectual property filing. These drawings must meet USPTO formal drawing requirements and accurately represent the invention's design and functional components.

Next Steps

  • Contact the patent attorney to confirm drawing specifications and format requirements
  • Engage a qualified patent illustrator to produce formal drawings per USPTO standards
  • Provide the illustrator with reference photos, dimensions, and technical details of the product
  • Review draft drawings for accuracy before final submission
  • Deliver finalized drawings to the patent attorney for inclusion in the application

Key Considerations

  • USPTO requires black-and-white line drawings with specific margin and labeling conventions
  • Drawings must show every feature described in the patent claims
  • Multiple views (front, side, top, exploded) are typically needed for utility patents
  • Turnaround from a patent illustrator is typically 1–3 weeks depending on complexity
