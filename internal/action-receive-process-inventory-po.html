1

Receive Shipment

  • Confirm delivery — verify carrier, number of boxes/pallets, and condition of packaging upon arrival
  • Match packing slip to PO — compare line items, quantities, and SKUs against the original purchase order
  • Check for damage — inspect all boxes for shipping damage; photograph any issues before opening
  • Sign delivery receipt — note any discrepancies or damage on the carrier's delivery paperwork
2

Inspect & Verify Product

  • Unbox and count — open each box, count individual units, and confirm total quantity matches PO
  • Verify SKUs — confirm each product matches the correct SKU (see reference table above)
  • Quality check — inspect covers for stitching quality, material integrity, and correct sizing
  • Flag discrepancies — document any shortages, overages, wrong items, or quality issues with photos
3

Update Records & Inventory

  • Update inventory count — add received quantities to the current inventory tracker for each SKU
  • Record lot/batch info — note the PO number, date received, and supplier batch details
  • Mark PO as received — update the purchase order status to "Received" or "Partially Received"
  • File packing slip — scan and save packing slip with the PO documentation
4

Stock & Organize Warehouse

  • Label products — ensure each cover is labeled with the correct Norris Utilities® SKU
  • Shelve by SKU — place covers in designated warehouse locations organized by SKU number
  • Update ready-to-ship count — confirm which units are available for immediate customer orders
  • Notify Aaron — send confirmation that inventory has been received, processed, and is ready for sale
