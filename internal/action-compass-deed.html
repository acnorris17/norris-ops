Action Item

Pull the 2018 Compass Bank → McKnight Deed from Probate Records

Created: April 12, 2026  |  Pending   High Priority

Task Details

Action Required
Retrieve the 2018 deed transferring property from Compass Bank to McKnight from the county probate court records.
Grantor
Compass Bank (now BBVA / PNC successor)
Grantee
McKnight
Year of Deed
2018
Record Source
County Probate Court Records
Assigned To
Aaron C. Norris

Steps to Complete

  • Access the county probate court records system (online portal or in-person at the courthouse).
  • Search grantor index for "Compass Bank" with a 2018 date range for deeds transferring to McKnight.
  • Search grantee index for "McKnight" with a 2018 date range to cross-reference the transfer from Compass Bank.
  • Locate the recorded deed instrument number, book, and page reference.
  • Pull a certified copy or download the scanned deed document from probate records.
  • Save the deed to file and confirm the legal description, parties, and recording date are correct.

Notes

Compass Bank was acquired by BBVA USA in 2019 and subsequently merged into PNC Bank in 2021. If the deed was recorded under "Compass Bank," search using that name for the 2018 timeframe. Also check under "BBVA Compass" as some records may have used that variant. The probate court deed records should list the original grantor name as it appeared at the time of recording.
