Photo Guidelines

Shoot in landscape orientation when possible. Ensure good natural lighting (early morning or overcast works best). Clean the truck before photos. Remove personal items from the cab. Include close-ups of any Norris Utilities® equipment installed. High resolution preferred (iPhone camera is fine).

Exterior Shots

  • Driver side full profile — Entire truck visible from left side, level with the ground
  • Passenger side full profile — Entire truck visible from right side, level with the ground
  • Front 3/4 angle — From front-left showing cab and service body together
  • Rear 3/4 angle — From rear-right showing the back of the service body
  • Rear straight-on — Showing tailgate, hitch, and rear compartments

Service Body Details

  • Compartments open — All side compartments open showing storage and organization
  • Bed/top of service body — Showing any mounted equipment, rails, or toolboxes
  • VIN plate / data sticker — Clear, readable photo of the VIN and any spec labels
  • Odometer / hour meter — Dashboard showing current mileage and engine hours
  • Any damage or wear — Document scratches, dents, rust, or wear areas for transparency
