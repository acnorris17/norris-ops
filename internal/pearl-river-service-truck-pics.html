Photo Tips
Lighting: Shoot in natural daylight. Avoid harsh midday shadows. Early morning or late afternoon light works best.

Angles: Keep the camera level with the truck. Stand back far enough to capture the full vehicle without distortion.

Resolution: Use the highest resolution setting on your phone camera. Landscape orientation for profile and front/rear shots.

Background: Park the truck in a clean, uncluttered area. Move any debris or other vehicles out of frame if possible.
