Source: reMarkable Action Items  |  Date Created: April 12, 2026  |  Owner: Aaron C. Norris
Open — Awaiting Photos

Pearl River has a service truck that needs to be photographed. These photos are needed for listing, quote package, trade evaluation, or marketing. Get clear, high-quality images covering all key angles of the truck.

Photos to Obtain
  • Front view — straight on, full truck visible, license plate area clear
  • Driver side — full profile showing body, compartments, and wheels
  • Passenger side — full profile, same level as driver side
  • Rear view — tailgate, rear compartments, hitch, and bumper
  • Service body compartments — all doors open, showing tool storage
  • Cab interior — dash, odometer/hour meter reading, seat condition
  • Engine compartment — clean shot with hood open
  • VIN plate and data sticker — close-up, legible
  • Tires — tread depth and sidewall condition on all four
  • Any damage, rust, dents, or wear — document everything honestly
Photo Guidelines
  • Shoot in daylight — no harsh shadows, overcast is ideal
  • Clean the truck before photos if possible
  • Landscape orientation for all exterior shots
  • Minimum 10 photos — more angles is always better
  • Send originals (not compressed) via AirDrop, email, or shared album
Next Step
