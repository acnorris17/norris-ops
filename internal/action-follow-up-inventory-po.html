Follow Up on Inventory Order from Last PO

Assigned To Aaron C. Norris
Date Created April 12, 2026
Status Awaiting Shipment Confirmation
Priority High — Inventory Dependent

Action Steps

  • Contact supplier to confirm current status of the last purchase order and request an updated ship date
  • Ask for tracking number or proof of shipment if order has already been dispatched
  • Verify all items and quantities on the PO match what is being shipped — no partial fills without approval
  • Confirm expected delivery date and coordinate receiving at the warehouse
  • If delayed, request a reason and escalate if the timeline impacts customer commitments
  • Update inventory tracking once shipment is confirmed en route

Talking Points for Supplier Call

  • Reference the specific PO number and date it was submitted
  • Express urgency — inventory levels depend on this shipment arriving on schedule
  • Ask whether the full order is shipping together or if any items are on backorder
  • Request email confirmation with tracking details once the order ships
  • If there is a delay, ask what the supplier needs from Norris Utilities® to expedite
