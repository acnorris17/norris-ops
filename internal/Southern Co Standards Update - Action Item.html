Action Item

Southern Co Standards Update

Created: April 12, 2026  |  Priority: High

Objective

Obtain the latest Southern Company material and equipment standards documentation. These standards govern approved products for all Southern Company subsidiaries — Alabama Power, Georgia Power, Mississippi Power, and Southern Company Gas — and directly impact which Norris Utilities® products can be specified on utility projects across the Southeast.

Action Steps

  • Contact Southern Company Supply Chain or Engineering Standards department to request current approved materials list and specification updates
  • Confirm FlexPro Armor bucket covers meet Southern Company's current bucket liner and protective cover specifications
  • Verify Samson Rope product lines against Southern Company's approved rope and rigging standards
  • Review any changes to equipment specifications that affect Skylift bucket truck configurations
  • Identify new product categories or specification changes that create sales opportunities
  • Update internal reference files with the latest standards revision numbers and effective dates

Their approved materials standards determine what products utilities in Alabama, Georgia, and Mississippi can purchase. Staying current on these standards ensures Norris Utilities® products remain specified and positions the company to capture new opportunities when specifications change.
