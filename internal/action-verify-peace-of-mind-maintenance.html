Confirm Peace of Mind Maintenance Program Requirement

Created: April 13, 2026

PENDING CONFIRMATION
Priority
Medium
Category
Maintenance / Service Agreement
Owner
Aaron C. Norris

Task Summary

Confirm whether the Peace of Mind maintenance program is required for the equipment or service agreement in question. This determination will impact ongoing service obligations, warranty coverage, and customer cost structure.

Key Questions to Resolve

  • Is the Peace of Mind maintenance program mandatory per the manufacturer or dealer agreement terms?
  • Does the customer's purchase or lease contract stipulate enrollment in the maintenance program?
  • What are the specific coverage benefits included in the Peace of Mind program (scheduled maintenance, parts, labor, emergency service)?
  • What is the cost to the customer if the program is required vs. optional?
  • Are there warranty implications if the customer declines the maintenance program?

Recommended Next Steps

  • Review the original equipment purchase or lease agreement for maintenance program language
  • Contact the manufacturer or dealer representative to confirm program requirements
  • Obtain written confirmation of whether enrollment is mandatory or optional
  • Document the program terms, coverage details, and pricing for the customer file
  • Communicate findings to the customer with a clear recommendation

Notes

If the Peace of Mind maintenance program is confirmed as required, ensure all terms are documented and the customer is informed of their obligations. If optional, prepare a cost-benefit summary to help guide the customer's decision.
