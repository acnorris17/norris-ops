Remove Pricing from Boss Online Store

Captured April 11, 2026 — Norris Utilities® reMarkable Action Item

What Needs to Happen

All pricing must be removed from the Boss Products online store. Visible pricing on the public-facing store creates channel conflict and undercuts dealer and distributor relationships. Pricing should only be available through direct quotes from Aaron or through authorized dealer channels.

Why This Matters

Public pricing on the Boss online store allows end-users and competitors to see cost structures, which weakens Norris Utilities® negotiating position and can create friction with existing dealer partners who expect pricing exclusivity. Removing visible pricing protects margins and keeps the sales process where it belongs — in direct conversations.

  3. 3 Confirm that wholesale/dealer pricing tiers are also hidden from any public-facing pages
  4. 4 Verify the changes are live on the Boss Products online store after they confirm completion
