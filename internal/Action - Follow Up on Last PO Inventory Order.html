Follow Up on Last PO Inventory Order

Urgent — Anxiously awaiting delivery. Follow up immediately to confirm status and ETA.
Action Type
PO Inventory Follow-Up
Priority
High — Awaiting Delivery
Date Created
April 13, 2026
Status
Pending — Needs Immediate Follow-Up

Action Details

The last purchase order for inventory was placed and is still outstanding. This order needs to be tracked down immediately to confirm it was received by the supplier, determine current fulfillment status, and get a firm expected delivery date.

This is time-sensitive — inventory levels depend on this order arriving. Do not wait for the supplier to reach out. Make the call, get a status update, and confirm when product will ship or arrive.

Next Steps

  1. Pull Up the Last PO Locate the most recent purchase order number, date submitted, and items ordered. Have this information ready before making the call.
  2. Call the Supplier Directly Do not email — call. Ask to speak with someone who can confirm the order status. Reference the PO number and date placed.
  3. Confirm Order Was Received Verify the supplier has the PO on file and that it has been entered into their system. If they do not have it, resend immediately.
  4. Get a Ship Date and Tracking Ask for a confirmed ship date. If it has already shipped, get the tracking number. If it has not shipped, ask why and when it will.
  5. Document the Response Write down the name of the person you spoke with, the date, and what they confirmed. Follow up with an email summarizing the conversation so there is a paper trail.
Important: If the supplier cannot provide a firm delivery date or the order has been delayed, escalate immediately. Consider whether a partial shipment or alternative sourcing is needed to keep inventory stocked. Do not let this sit — follow up daily until product is confirmed delivered.
