Steps to Complete

  1. 1 Confirm with Trinity the specific Manitex crane model, tonnage, and boom length they need
  2. 2 Contact Manitex or the authorized Manitex dealer to get current pricing on the requested unit (new or used, confirm availability)
  3. 3 Structure the RPO terms: monthly rental rate, term length, percentage of payments applied to purchase, buyout price at end of term
  4. 4 Apply Norris Utilities® equipment margin (12–15%) to the deal pricing
  5. 5 Calculate shipping/delivery costs using Ben’s Formula: ROUNDUP(ROUNDUP(RegCost, 0) × 1.10, 0)
  6. 6 Prepare and send the formal RPO quote package to Trinity with all terms, specs, and pricing
