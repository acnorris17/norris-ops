Negotiation Game Plan
1

Open at $900,000

Submit the offer at $900,000 with a strong earnest money deposit to demonstrate seriousness. Include standard contingencies for inspection, appraisal, and financing. Show you are a qualified, decisive buyer.

2

Support With Comps

Back the $900K offer with comparable sales data for 5,000–8,000 sq ft homes sold in the area within the last 12 months. Present price-per-square-foot analysis to justify the offer as fair market value.

3

Leverage Inspection Findings

At 7,098 sq ft, deferred maintenance costs are amplified. Use documented inspection findings to negotiate price reductions or seller-paid repair credits. Roof, HVAC, and foundation are key areas.

4

Offer Flexible Closing Terms

Flexibility on closing timeline can be worth $10K–$25K to a motivated seller. Offer to close on the seller's preferred timeline as a concession instead of raising price.

5

Counter Strategy

If seller counters above $950K, respond with a maximum of $925K–$935K and include the inspection report as justification. Hold firm at $950K ceiling unless appraisal supports higher value.

6

Secure Financing Early

Contact Patrick Lavette at Renasant Bank immediately. Secure pre-approval letter for up to $975K to show full qualification. A pre-approved buyer carries more weight than a cash-uncertain one.

Strategy Notes

The $900,000 offer on 4505 Buttewoods represents $126.80 per square foot for 7,098 sq ft of living space.
