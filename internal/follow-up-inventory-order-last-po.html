Follow-Up Checklist
  • Pull the most recent purchase order number and review line items ordered
  • Contact the supplier to confirm order acknowledgment and current status
  • Verify expected ship date and carrier tracking information if available
  • Confirm quantities match what was originally ordered — flag any backorders or substitutions
  • Update internal inventory tracker with confirmed delivery timeline
  • If delayed, escalate with supplier contact and document revised ETA
Context & Notes

This follow-up is tied to the last purchase order placed for inventory replenishment. The goal is to make sure nothing has fallen through the cracks — confirm the supplier received and acknowledged the PO, verify quantities and delivery dates, and flag any discrepancies before product is needed for customer orders. If any items are backordered or delayed, get a firm revised date and adjust planning accordingly.
