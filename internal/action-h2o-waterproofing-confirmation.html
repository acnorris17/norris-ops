Call H2O Waterproofing — Get Written Confirmation of Work

Captured April 13, 2026 — reMarkable Action Item
Follow up with H2O Waterproofing to obtain written documentation confirming the scope and completion of work performed.

What to Do

Call H2O Waterproofing and request written confirmation of the work that was performed. This documentation should be on their letterhead or an official form and include the details of what was done, when it was completed, and any warranty or guarantee information.

Checklist

  • Call H2O Waterproofing and speak with the project lead or owner
  • Request a written letter or invoice confirming the work completed
  • Confirm the document includes: scope of work, dates of service, and materials used
  • Ask for any applicable warranty or guarantee details in writing
  • Request the confirmation be sent via email to acnorris@norrisutilities.com
  • Save the written confirmation to records once received

Why This Matters

Having written confirmation protects Norris Utilities® in the event of future disputes, insurance claims, or warranty issues. Verbal agreements are not enforceable without documentation. Get it in writing.
