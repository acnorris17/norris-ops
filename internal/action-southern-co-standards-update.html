Southern Co Standards Update

Captured April 13, 2026 — from reMarkable Notes
◆
Objective

Obtain the latest Southern Company approved vendor/materials standards list. This is critical for ensuring all Norris Utilities® product offerings — including FlexPro Armor bucket covers, Samson Rope, and equipment lines — align with Southern Company's current specifications and approved materials requirements.

✓
Action Steps
  1. Contact Southern Company procurement or standards department to request the current approved vendor and materials standards documentation.
  2. Reach out to existing Southern Co contacts (field operations, purchasing agents) who may have direct access to the updated standards list.
  3. Check with Southern Co member utilities — Alabama Power, Georgia Power, Mississippi Power — for any regional standards variations.
  4. Review Norris Utilities® product catalog against updated standards to confirm compliance for FlexPro Armor, Samson Rope, and all equipment lines.
  5. Identify any new product approval opportunities — if standards have changed, determine whether Norris Utilities® can submit products for inclusion.
  6. Document findings and update internal records with the latest approved standards version and any action items for product compliance.
ⓘ
Why This Matters

Southern Company and its operating subsidiaries represent a major segment of the Southeast utility market. Their approved materials standards dictate what products their crews and contractors can purchase and use in the field.

Being listed on or compliant with Southern Co standards opens the door to direct sales across Alabama Power, Georgia Power, Mississippi Power, and Southern Linc — all within the Norris Utilities® core service territory.

An updated standards list also helps Aaron position FlexPro Armor and other products when quoting to co-ops and municipalities that follow Southern Co specifications.
