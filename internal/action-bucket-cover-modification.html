Modify Shipped Bucket Cover — Resize Smaller & Reship

A FlexPro Armor bucket cover has been shipped that needs modification. The cover must be resized smaller to properly fit the customer's bucket and then reshipped. This action tracks the full process from retrieval through final delivery.

Action Steps
1

Coordinate Return or Retrieval of Current Cover

Contact the customer to arrange return of the oversized bucket cover. Provide a prepaid return shipping label or coordinate local pickup if applicable.

2

Confirm Correct Dimensions with Customer

Verify the exact bucket measurements needed. Determine whether the customer needs the 1.5-Man (28" × 34") size or a custom modification of the shipped cover. Get the bucket make, model, and year if not already on file.

3

Submit Modification to Production

Send the cover to production with exact specifications for the smaller size. Include all measurements, any custom fit notes, and the customer's bucket details.

4

Quality Check Modified Cover

Inspect the resized cover before shipping. Verify dimensions match the customer's requirements. Check all stitching, material integrity, and FlexPro Armor quality standards.

5

Reship to Customer

Ship the modified cover with tracking. Send tracking number to the customer. Use Ben's Shipping Formula for any additional shipping costs: Customer Cost = ROUNDUP(ROUNDUP(RegCost, 0) × 1.10, 0).

6

Follow Up & Confirm Fit

Contact the customer after delivery to confirm the modified cover fits properly. Document final measurements and any notes for future orders from this customer.

Key Reminders

  • FlexPro Armor covers are handmade in the USA — modification turnaround depends on production schedule
  • Competitor "24×48" is equivalent to our NU-BC-2851 (28" × 51"); their "24×34" matches our NU-BC-2834 (28" × 34")
  • FlexPro Armor pricing: 50% margin on all covers (Cost ÷ 0.50 = Customer Price)
  • If the customer needs a completely new cover instead of a modification, process as a new order under the correct SKU
  • Document this modification for quality tracking — helps prevent future sizing mismatches
