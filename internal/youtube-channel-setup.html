Action Item

Set Up YouTube Channel & Highway Video

Launch the Norris Utilities® YouTube presence with branded channel setup and the highway driving video as the first published content piece.

Phase 1 — YouTube Channel Creation
1

Create the YouTube Channel

Sign into Google with the Norris Utilities® business account and create a Brand Account channel named Norris Utilities.

  • Channel handle: @NorrisUtilities
  • Category: Science & Technology
  • Location: Birmingham, Alabama
High Priority
2

Upload Channel Branding

Apply the Norris Utilities® visual identity across the channel.

  • Profile picture: Phoenix Icon® logo (800x800px minimum)
  • Banner image: Blue-to-cyan gradient header with "NORRIS UTILITIES" and tagline (2560x1440px)
  • Watermark: Phoenix Icon® shield at low opacity for on-video branding
3

Complete Channel Details

Fill in the About section and all channel metadata for discoverability.

  • Description: "Norris Utilities® — third-generation utility equipment dealer serving 20+ states. FlexPro Armor bucket covers, Samson Rope, Skylift bucket trucks, and Bay Shore Systems drill rigs. A Legacy of Commitment®."
  • Contact email: acnorris@norrisutilities.com
  • Website link: www.NorrisUtilities.com
  • Business phone: 205-500-1343
4

Configure Channel Settings

Set up the channel for a professional, brand-consistent experience.

  • Enable custom URL once eligibility requirements are met
  • Set default upload settings: Title prefix "Norris Utilities |", tags include "utility equipment", "bucket covers", "FlexPro Armor", "lineman equipment"
  • Set default visibility to Unlisted until each video is reviewed and approved
  • Turn on comment moderation to hold comments for review
Phase 2 — Highway Video Upload
5

Prepare the Highway Video File

Locate the highway driving video and prepare it for upload.

  • Confirm video resolution is 1080p or higher
  • Trim any dead time at the start or end of the clip
  • Add a Norris Utilities® branded intro card (3–5 seconds) with logo and tagline
  • Add a branded end screen with contact info and subscribe prompt
High Priority
6

Upload and Tag the Video

Publish the highway video as the first piece of content on the channel.

  • Title: "Norris Utilities | Highway Drive — Utility Equipment You Can Count On"
  • Description: Include company overview, product lines, website, and contact info
  • Tags: utility equipment, bucket trucks, FlexPro Armor, Samson Rope, lineman tools, Birmingham Alabama, Norris Utilities
  • Thumbnail: Custom branded thumbnail with blue gradient and video still
  • Category: Science & Technology
7

Review and Go Live

Before switching to Public, verify everything looks right on both desktop and mobile.

  • Preview the video on the channel page
  • Confirm branding, description, links, and thumbnail are correct
  • Switch visibility from Unlisted to Public
  • Share the video link on the Norris Utilities® website and social media

Channel Naming Guidance

The YouTube channel name should be Norris Utilities (no LLC, no registered symbol in the display name — YouTube does not render special characters well in channel names). The registered symbol should appear in the channel description and video descriptions where appropriate.
