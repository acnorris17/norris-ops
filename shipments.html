Order-to-Invoice Workflow

  1. Order received (email / Telegram / verbal)
  2. SD generated, customer confirms
  3. Pick & pack at NU stock OR Boss drop-ship
  4. UPS pickup → tracking captured
  5. Shipping cost computed (Ben's / Dealer's formula)
  6. QB invoice created (CB pastes from Copy-for-QB)
  7. Invoice sent to customer
  8. Sheet writeback: qb_invoice + sent + verified flagged

Payment Method Rules

  • Direct Net 30/Receipt — corporate A/P, no CC fee
  • Direct CC — add 4% processing fee line
  • Dealer ACH — Wayne Abadie, no CC fee
  • Dealer CC — apply 4% if charged to card
  • LineTec branches — Alexandria=A/P, Guthrie=CC
  • Brink — Travis Fischer ACH parent; Chad Christian CC
  • ULCS — always 4% CC

Shipping Cost Formula

Ben's (direct):
ROUNDUP(ROUNDUP(RegCost,0) × 1.10, 0)

Dealer's:
ROUNDUP(ROUNDUP(RegCost,0) × 1.05, 0)

Multi-tracking: apply per-package, then sum. Never sum reg costs first.
