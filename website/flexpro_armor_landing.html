Why FlexPro Armor?

Rigid Top Panel

The reinforced rigid top panel prevents sagging and pooling, keeping water, ice, and debris from collecting on the cover. Your bucket stays clean and ready for the next job.

Flexi-Rigid Collapsible Design

Collapses flat in seconds for compact storage and transport. The flexible sidewalls fold down while the rigid top maintains its shape, so the cover is always ready to deploy.

Water-Shedding Construction

Engineered to shed rain, sleet, and snow on contact. The angled rigid top and treated fabric work together so water runs off instead of soaking through to the bucket.

Hand Made in the USA

Every FlexPro Armor cover is hand-crafted in the United States. No mass production, no overseas shortcuts. Built by skilled hands with pride and attention to detail.
