Who We Are

More Than a Utility Company.
A Family Standard.

Norris Utilities LLC was founded on November 9, 2021, in Birmingham, Alabama — but our story didn't begin there. It began decades earlier, in the calloused hands and unshakeable work ethic of a man named Wayne Norris, whose devotion to craft, character, and community laid the foundation that Aaron C. Norris built this company upon. We are a minority-owned electrical infrastructure and utility services firm, and we carry every job, every crew, and every client relationship with the same pride that has defined the Norris name across three generations.

Our Mission

Deliver Excellence. Protect Communities.

We exist to deliver safe, reliable, and forward-thinking utility infrastructure services — from underground electrical distribution to substation maintenance and beyond. Our commitment to quality is not a company policy. It is a family inheritance.

Our Vision

A Name Synonymous with Reliability.

Norris Utilities is building toward a future where our name appears on every major infrastructure project in the Southeast — and beyond. We are scaling a legacy, not just a business, ensuring every generation of Norris leadership leaves the industry better than they found it.
