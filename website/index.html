"A Legacy of Commitment®"

Built on Heritage

Norris Utilities® carries forward a family tradition that spans three generations in the utility industry. Founded in November 2021 by Aaron C. Norris in Birmingham, Alabama, the company was built on decades of firsthand knowledge passed down from his father and grandfather — men who spent their careers on the line and in the field.

That heritage drives everything we do. From handpicking every product line we represent to personally supporting each customer relationship, Norris Utilities® operates with the conviction that your equipment partner should know the work as well as you do.

We don't just sell equipment — we earn the business. Every call, every order, every delivery.

Today, Norris Utilities® serves electric cooperatives, investor-owned utilities, municipalities, and utility contractors across more than 20 states — with the same hands-on, no-shortcuts approach that built this family's reputation.

Serving 20+ States

From our home base in Birmingham, Alabama, Norris Utilities® covers a growing territory spanning the Southeast, Mid-Atlantic, and Midwest. Our reach continues to expand as more utilities and contractors discover the Norris difference.

Whether you need equipment delivered to a job site in the Carolinas or product support in Texas, we are your single point of contact — responsive, knowledgeable, and always available.
