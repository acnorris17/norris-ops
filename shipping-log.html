📋 Order-to-Invoice Workflow

  1. Order received — assign SD# and enter in Shipping Log
  2. Product pulled and packed at warehouse
  3. Carrier scheduled — update Carrier column
  4. Package scanned — enter Tracking # immediately
  5. Shipping invoice received — enter Ship Cost
  6. Calculate Billed Customer using formula: ROUNDUP(ROUNDUP(RegCost, 0) × 1.10, 0)
  7. Only after Steps 4 + 5 complete → create QB Invoice
  8. Enter QB Invoice # and update Status to Invoiced

💳 Payment Method Rules

  • Net 30 — Standard terms for established utility accounts
  • Credit Card — Always apply +4% processing surcharge
  • CC surcharge must be itemized on QB invoice as separate line
  • Flag all CC orders visually with ⚠ CC +4% badge
  • Prepaid orders — mark as "Prepaid" and note payment date

📦 Shipping Cost Formula

  • Customer Cost = ROUNDUP(ROUNDUP(RegCost, 0) × 1.10, 0)
  • Round the carrier's regular cost up to the nearest dollar first
  • Multiply by 1.10 (10% handling markup)
  • Round result up to the nearest dollar
  • Example: Carrier charges $42.37 → $43 → $47.30 → $48.00 billed
